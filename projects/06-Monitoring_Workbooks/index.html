Sample KQL Query:

// Find resources with high CPU usage
AzureMetrics
| where ResourceProvider == "MICROSOFT.COMPUTE"
| where MetricName == "Percentage CPU"
| where Average > 80
| summarize AvgCPU = avg(Average) by Resource
| order by AvgCPU desc
| take 10
